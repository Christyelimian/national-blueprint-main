## MEDIA GUIDELINES FOR TEAM

### Photography Style Guide
```
DO:
✓ Professional, documentary style
✓ Well-lit, high resolution
✓ Showing actual work/progress
✓ Aerial/infrastructure views
✓ Technical/planning imagery
✓ Neutral, professional backgrounds

DON'T:
❌ Stock photos with fake scenarios
❌ Overly staged or posed
❌ Smiling families/lifestyle imagery
❌ Property marketing style
❌ Low resolution or amateur quality
❌ Cluttered or distracting backgrounds
```

### Graphic Design Style Guide
```
Colors:
- Primary: Navy #1A2332
- Accent: Gold #B8860B
- Supporting: Charcoal #2C3E50, Stone #D3CFCB

Typography in Graphics:
- Headings: Serif font (Playfair Display)
- Body: Sans-serif (Inter)
- Data labels: Sans-serif, clear

Style:
- Minimal, clean designs
- Generous white space
- Professional data visualization
- No decorative flourishes
- Focus on clarity over creativity
```
