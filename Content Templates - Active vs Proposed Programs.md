## Usage Guidelines

### 1. **Status Clarity**
- Always lead with clear status badge
- Distinguish between "executing" vs "planning"
- Be transparent about contingencies

### 2. **Data Precision**
- Active programs: Real-time data, specific dates
- Proposed programs: Ranges, targets, "proposed" language
- Hybrid programs: Clear separation of active vs proposed

### 3. **Investor Focus**
- Financial structure prominently displayed
- Risk assessment clearly articulated
- Return metrics provided where available

### 4. **Government Emphasis**
- Partnership status clearly stated
- Regulatory pathway outlined
- Contingencies tied to government actions

### 5. **Scalability Indication**
- Show how program fits into larger national strategy
- Indicate replication potential
- Provide template for similar programs

---

**Note**: These templates are designed for institutional audiences (World Bank, IFC, development finance institutions, sovereign wealth funds, impact investors). Language should be precise, data-driven, and risk-aware while maintaining confidence in execution capability.
